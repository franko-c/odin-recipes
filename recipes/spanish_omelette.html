Spanish Omelette

Description:

It is often decreed that the only ingredients (other than eggs of course) proper to a Spanish omelette are onions and potatoes, but I like a touch of red from some chopped peppers.

I am sorry if this offends — and not only the Spanish — but you could speed this up by using some drained, canned new potatoes instead of cooking real ones.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ½ pound potatoes, thinly sliced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 large eggs

Optional Ingredients:

  • 2 medium tomatoes - peeled, seeded, and coarsely chopped
  • 2 green onions, chopped
